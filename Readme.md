## ✨ Features

- 🔒 **100% Secure & Local** – All Excel parsing and calculations happen right in your browser. No personal data ever leaves your device.
- ⚡ **Instant Search & Process** – Skip the manual lookup. Enter your Registration Number and instantly extract your specific grades from massive university spreadsheets.
- 📊 **Smart SGPA Generator** – Automatically calculates SGPA, Total Credits, and Credits Cleared using standard university credit-point algorithms.
- 📑 **Official Grade Sheet UI** – Generates a professional, iCloudEMS-style digital grade sheet complete with university branding and student details.
- 🚨 **Smart Backlog Detection** – Automatically detects and highlights failed or absent subjects (F/M grades) with clear system warnings.
- 📈 **Dedicated CGPA Calculator** – A standalone tool to seamlessly add semester-wise SGPAs and total credits to calculate your Cumulative GPA.
- 📤 **Multiple Export Options** – Save your generated grade sheet offline as a high-resolution **PDF** or as an **Image**.
- 💬 **One-Click WhatsApp Share** – Instantly share a formatted text summary of your semester performance securely to any WhatsApp contact.
- 🔍 **Algorithm Transparency** – Built-in modal to view the exact mathematical formulas used for SGPA and CGPA calculations.
- 📂 **Official Templates Access** – Quick access to verified university Excel templates directly via Google Drive integration.
- 🎨 **Modern Interface** – A clean, glassmorphism-inspired dark mode UI built for speed, responsiveness, and simplicity.
